üè• 5. Applications of Nuclear Chemistry

Medical Applications:

  • Radioiodine Therapy: I-131 for thyroid treatment (8-day half-life)
  • PET Scans: F-18 fluorodeoxyglucose for cancer detection
  • External Beam Therapy: Co-60 gamma rays for tumor treatment
  • Brachytherapy: Radioactive seeds implanted near tumors
  • Technetium-99m: Most used medical isotope for imaging

Other Applications:

  • Carbon-14 Dating: Artifacts up to 50,000 years old
  • Nuclear Power: ~10% of world's electricity
  • Food Irradiation: Cobalt-60 kills bacteria
  • Smoke Detectors: Americium-241 ionization
  • Industrial Gauging: Thickness measurements
